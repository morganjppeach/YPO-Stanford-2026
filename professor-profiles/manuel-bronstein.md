## 📖 COMPREHENSIVE DOSSIER

### Background
Born/raised in Caracas, Venezuela; **BS Electrical Engineering, Universidad Simón Bolívar; MBA, UC Berkeley Haas.** Began at **P&G** (assistant brand manager) — the rare product leader with brand-management + engineering DNA.

### Career (5 platforms at scale)
- **Microsoft Xbox (2003–2010):** Xbox LIVE incubation; "1vs100" interactive TV-game show; partnerships (Netflix, Facebook, ESPN) — living-room product design.
- **Zynga (2010–2014):** VP Product (FarmVille era) — viral growth, retention loops, virtual economies.
- **YouTube (2014–2018):** VP Product — mobile/desktop/living-room apps, live streaming, growth, community; creator-platform dynamics.
- **Google Assistant (2018–2021):** VP Product — conversational AI at global scale (vs. Alexa).
- **Roblox (2021–2025):** **CPO** — product, design, data science, BD, partnerships; launched generative-AI creation tools + new creator monetization (2024). Departed Sep 2025 (advisory through ~Apr 2026).

### Boards & investing
NYT Company board (since Oct 2021; Nominating & Governance); Match Group board (announced Feb 2026); angel/advisor since 2011 (EdTech, gaming; e.g., TollBit).

### Signature ideas ("10 Counterintuitive Principles")
Build fast & learn faster; first-principles thinking; radical simplification; systems thinking for virtual economies (trust & safety by design); platforms need a dual creator/consumer strategy; deliberately scale culture to keep startup speed.

### AI perspective
AI in consumer products is an **acceleration problem, not invention** — make existing workflows faster/easier (Assistant = conversational retrieval; Roblox = accessible creation); pair acceleration with trust infrastructure.

### Distinctive style
Translator across domains (engagement loops Zynga→YouTube; creator dynamics YouTube→Roblox; AI Google→Roblox). Framework-first, example-rich, challenges conventional wisdom.

### Likely executive Q&A (grounded)
- **"Build vs. buy vs. partner?"** Build what differentiates the core platform; partner for distribution/reach; buy when time-to-market is critical and integration risk manageable.
- **"Integrate GenAI without breaking what works?"** Start as an accelerator for power users; measure quality/velocity and trust-and-safety; expand when metrics are stable.
- **"Preserve startup culture at 50→500+?"** Small autonomous squads with clear ownership; decisions close to user data; radical simplification to fight process bloat.
- **"Growth vs. creator monetization?"** Interdependent systems, not a tradeoff — creators attract users; users fund creators.
- **"Org structure for product+platform+creator tools?"** CPO owns the full user-facing stack with embedded data science/design.
