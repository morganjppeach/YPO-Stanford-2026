### Likely executive Q&A (grounded)
- **"Secure AI agents using external tools/APIs?"** Real-time monitoring of agent-tool interactions, zero-trust per tool invocation, audit trails (AI Defense agentic protections; Zero Trust Access for agents).
- **"Platform vs. point solutions?"** Invest in a control plane (policy, observability, governance) spanning AI apps, not per-use-case tools.
- **"AI-security maturity model?"** Secure models → secure AI supply chain → secure agentic workflows (don't skip steps).
- **"Innovation speed vs. governance?"** Not a tradeoff — "shift-left" security for AI builds the confidence that speeds adoption.
- **"Lessons from two exits?"** Spot the new abstraction early, build security for it, define the category, partner with top VCs, position for platform acquisition — timing the paradigm shift is the risk.
- **"Is 'agentic AI' real or hype?"** If your AI takes actions (not just generates text), you're already in the agentic era and need agentic-era security.
